About me

I'm Anmol and I'm a

Product Manager an obsessive learner. My Terrain of action is IIT (BHU), Varanasi, where I consider myself an explorer, whether it's new people, new hobbies, new learnings, or whatnot. With all the exploration, I came up with the conclusion that I want to be a Product Person and hence worked with multiple startups in Fintech, Ecommerce Marketplace, Edtech, and Healthcare to strengthen my skillset. I am an avid follower of the startup, business, and tech ecosystem and obsessed with how different products are solving the everyday problems of users and want to contribute to the same.Apart from this, My daily life revolves around collecting thoughts on the Product, other than that hobbies include driving, cycling, networking, cooking, and gardening.
